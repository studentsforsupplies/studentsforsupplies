ABOUT US

Our student organization, called Supplies for Students (S4S), is based in Maryland and made up of over 40 students from various public high schools. When the school year begins, we plan to hold monthly meetings with all members to discuss upcoming events and distribute roles. As for advocacy, we created this student coalition to involve our community in providing a quality education to the next generation. We hope to accomplish this through events to raise awareness, educational presentations at schools, and fundraisers or drives. Currently, we have already planned a couple basic fundraisers involving local restaurants and bake sales to get us started. As the school year begins, we plan to expand our organization in order to host larger fundraisers and supply drives.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to advocate for a quality education, fundraise for statewide educational initiatives, and provide adequate supplies for students. We hope that we can impact the lives of children and supply them with the necessary materials to succeed later in life. Both my fellow members and I recognize the importance of education and how more access to instructional material can increase opportunities for a student to perform better academically.
